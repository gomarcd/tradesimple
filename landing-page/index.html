TradeSimple

The trading journal built for Wealthsimple

A modern open-source webapp that connects directly to Wealthsimple via its GraphQL API, giving you powerful insights for your trading and investment journey.

Features

Everything you need to track your trades

TradeSimple is designed specifically for Wealthsimple users, with features that other platforms don't offer.

Direct Connection

Connect directly to Wealthsimple via its GraphQL API for reliable data access without third-party aggregators.

Multiple Accounts

Connect and manage multiple Wealthsimple accounts in one place with a comprehensive view of your portfolios.

Performance Analytics

Track your trading performance with detailed metrics including P&L, win rate, and returns compared to the market.

Trade Journaling

Document your trades with detailed notes and insights to help improve your strategy over time.

Data Privacy

Self-hosted solution means your financial data stays private and secure with no third-party access.

Open Source

Freely audit, modify, and contribute to the codebase. Built by traders for traders with full transparency and no hidden costs.
